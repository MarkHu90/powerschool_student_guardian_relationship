Update Demographic Information

YK Pao School is required to have accurate and up to date information at all times regarding every student. Please assist us by verifying all records are correct for your family. Please be aware that at any time you may update demographic information through your PowerSchool Parent Portal. For accuracy of data, this is the only acceptable method for modifying records. Changes are not allowed in person, by phone, or email.

Once submitted, please allow up to 3 business days for the information to be approved and processed. Some fields may require verification with proof of legal documents. If this is the case, you may be contacted to provide a copy of the necessary documents by our Student Affairs Office.

为了确保每位学生在包校信息系统中的个人信息及时有效，现请大家协助核实相关信息。请知晓如果信息需要修改，您需要登录PowerSchool家长门户网站进行相关操作，为了保证信息的准确度，学校将不接受电话和邮件的方式申请。

您递交修改信息的申请后，我们将在3个工作日内审核并更新。某些个人信息的更新修改需要提供相关法律文件证明，学生处老师会与您联系！
